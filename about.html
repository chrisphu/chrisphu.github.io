Hello!

Who am I?

My name is Chris Phu and I am a mechanical engineer.

I graduated from the University of Arkansas with a BSME and am currently employed at Honeywell FM&T as a simulation analyst. A majority of my job includes modeling physical phenomena using computer-aided design (CAD) and finite element analysis (FEA) software.

In my free time I enjoy programming games in Unity, 3D printing, and playing video games/board games.

What is this website for?

While I have always enjoyed working on problems and projects, for awhile I didn't have a good medium for capturing all of my thoughts. When settling on an option, I almost certainly would have gone with an existing website/blogging service if not for GitHub Pages being free for public repos.
